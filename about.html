Our Story

Equipping People for Purpose Through Worship and the Word

Worship Mandate Ministries (WMM) is a Christ-centered worship ministry raising people who know God deeply and live out divine purpose.

Founded by Minister Seun Dede, WMM is committed to life transformation through spirit-lifting worship, sound teaching, and practical discipleship.

Through conferences, worship gatherings, growth resources, and the Raising Sounds Initiative (RSI), we are training a generation of worshippers who carry Heaven's sound wherever they go.
